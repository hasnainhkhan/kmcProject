About Us

The University is fast expanding both in terms of facilities and students enrolled. The University has separate boys’ and girls’ hostels that provide convenient accommodation to the students with in house mess facility. The boys’ hostel has a capacity of around 300 students, and the girls’ hostel also offers an accommodation for 300 students. To make the stay of the students pleasant and enjoyable, various activities, events and celebrations are organized within the hostels on a regular basis. Both the hostels are enabled with free wi-fi services and indoor games. CCTV cameras have also been installed to maintain safety and security. The allotment of rooms is made as per the rules, guidelines and relevant regulations of the University. The hostel facility is available at quite an affordable cost, i.e. INR 12650/-(Annual) for new student and 11650/- for renewal at the time of allotment in double occupancy. For Ph. D. Students it is 23000/- in single occupancy and 14000/- (Annual) in double occupancy. Besides this, students will have to pay the mess charges for availing the mess facility, that is compulsory for every resident. Khwaja Moinuddin Chishti Language University, located in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, has separate hostels for boys and girls The boys’ hostel has a capacity of around 300 students and offers in-house mess facilities. The hostel is equipped with Personal Wi-Fi services and indoor games. CCTV cameras have also been installed (but not working properly) to maintain safety and security 1. The annual hostel fee for new students is INR 12650/- for double occupancy and INR 23000/- for single occupancy 1.
